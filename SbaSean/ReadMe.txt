A basic bank application.
The application has 3 pages
A login in page where you can log in to your account
A create account page where you can create an account
A view account page where you view your account information
One the view account page you can add and remove money from your account
You can't remove more than what you have
Password must have 1 lowercase letter, 1 uppercase letter, a number and a special character
Start by opening the sba.html file
